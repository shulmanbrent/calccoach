Nakia Rimmer

Nakia Rimmer is a Senior lecturer and Associate Director of Undergraduate Mathematics at the University of Pennsylvania. Born and raised in Cleveland, OH, Mr. Rimmer has a BS in Mathematics from Youngstown State University, an MA in Mathematics from the University of Pennsylvania, and an MS in Applied Mathematics from the University of Delaware.

CalcCoach.com was born out of a need to provide his course materials (lecture notes, practice exams and actual exams with full solutions, premium videos on exam level questions or higher, and lecture videos) to students not taking the course with him as the professor. Instead of student waiting until he taught the course, they can now have access to all his resources.
